In the name of God

-  `netstat`
   -  `-n, --numeric` be numerical, instead of trying to resolve host, port and user names.
      -  `--numeric-hosts`
      -  `--numeric-ports`
      -  `--numeric-users`
   -  `-c, --continuous` watch (every second).
   -  `-e, --extend` additional information. Twice for maximal info.
   -  `-o, --timers`
   -  `-p, --program` include PID.
   -  `-l, --listening` only listening sockets (omitted by default).
   -  `-a, --all` both listening and non-listening.
